# Content Standards

## Minimum Requirements for All Blog Posts

### 1. Length
- **Minimum: 1,200 words**
- Target: 1,200-1,800 words
- Word count must be verified before publishing

### 2. Structure
- **At least 3-4 H2 headings** per article
- H2s break up content and are critical for SEO

### 3. Keyword Placement in Headings
- **At least 1 H2 per article must contain a target keyword**
- Weekly: at least 1 post per site must have a long-tail keyword in the H1 title

### 4. Images
- **At least 1 image per post** (when relevant)
- Must include descriptive alt tags with keywords
- Format: `![keyword-rich description](image-url)`
- Alt tags should describe the image AND include target keywords

### 5. Long-Tail Keywords
- Include 2-3 long-tail keyword phrases naturally in body

### 6. Keyword Placement
- Title (H1): Primary keyword
- First 100 words: Primary keyword
- H2 headings: At least 1 with target keyword
- Throughout body: Natural keyword density (1-2%)
- Last 100 words: Primary keyword

## Homepage Requirements
- Minimum: **2,000 words** (was 1,500)
- Must include all primary keywords
- **Multiple images recommended** (at least 2-3: headshot, location, work)
- Person schema with sameAs (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- LSI keywords throughout

## About Page Requirements
- Minimum: **2,000 words** (was 1,500)
- Full biography
- **Multiple images recommended**
- Person schema with sameAs
